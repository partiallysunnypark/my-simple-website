About the Book

"The Land Beyond the Pages" invites young readers on a magical journey through whimsical landscapes, where charming characters and hidden treasures await within every beautifully crafted page.

This captivating book promises to inspire and entertain children, making it perfect for bedtime stories or an adventure into a fantastical world. Each drawing and story is a unique glimpse into the author's vibrant imagination.

About the Author & Illustrator

This delightful creation is the remarkable work of Sandra Chen, who started this book at the tender age of 8 years old and devoted an entire year to bringing both the writing and illustrations to life. Her boundless imagination and creativity shine through every page.

The drawings beautifully bring Sandra's vivid visions to life, making "The Land Beyond the Pages" a testament to the magic that lies within every child's heart.

Special thanks to Bright Moon Art Studio for their support and guidance on the illustrations.
